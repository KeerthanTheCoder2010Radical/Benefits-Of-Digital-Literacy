Benefits Of Digital Literacy

The biggest benefit of Digital Literacy is the development of the four communication skills ; writing, reading, listening, and speaking skills. Digital Literacy also has the potential to generate more excitement in learning. This point is most applicable for children.


What is digital Literacy?
